The EMMA Woodshop Tenants

We believe in the handmade over the mass produced and unique over generic. Every piece that comes from the EMMA Woodshop is truly one of a kind.

We believe in re-purposing and re-using. Wood never loses it's beauty, it just has to be brought back to the forefront. We use re-purposed lumber and hardware to create unique and stylish pieces.

We believe in creating pieces that stand the test of time. We use tried and true methods to produce quality that can be passed down for generations to come.
